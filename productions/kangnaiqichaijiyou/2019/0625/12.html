详细描述

 
CI-4   康耐琪“劲能”超护燃气发动机专用机油 （CNG/LPG）
CNG&LPG   Super protection gas engine oil
动力持久      一路保护
产品配方：合成基础油、CI-4复合剂、美国VDT特种分子、分散型粘指剂、其他辅料等，所有原辅材料全部进口采购。
产品性能：超越API  CI-4 ，满足所有达到欧Ⅳ及以下排放标准的高性能商务运输车的燃气发动机润滑要求，并提供非凡保护。优异的氧化安定性，从基础油与添加剂两方面着手改进油品的高温稳定性和抗氧化能力；平衡的新型低灰配方；独特成分形成阀门保护膜等科技的运用，有效解决燃气发动机“两高一难”，即燃烧室高温，尾气中氮氧化物含量高和难润滑的问题。超强动力使车辆运行更轻松畅快；超强的清洁功能，防止活塞沉积物产生，确保润滑系统无油泥、积碳，光洁如新；出色的烟炱控制能力，有效减少烟炱污染油品，减少磨损、延长油品寿命；优异的抗磨和抗氧化能力，即使在恶劣工况条件下也能提供极佳的润滑保护，延长发动机寿命；良好的低温流动性，减少启动时间和磨损、节省燃油；与EGR、SCR良好的相容性，保护尾排处理系统，减少有害气体排放，保护环境。
Product performance:  Beyond API CI-4 which meets all the requirements of gas engine lubrication of Euro IV emission standards or following emission standards high performance commercial transport vehicles and provides special protection. It has excellent oxidation stability, from two aspects of the base oil and additives  to improve the  high temperature stability of oil products and anti-oxidation capacity, balance new low ash formula, it also has unique components to form the application of valve protective film and other technologies, it effectively solve the problem of "two highs and one difficulty”of gas engines which is the problem of combustion chamber with high temperature, high content of nitrogen oxide in the rear gas and difficult lubrication. The super power makes the vehicles run more quickly, the super cleaning function  prevents the production of sediment piston which guarantees  the lubrication system of no sludge and carbon deposition, bright and clean as ever, excellent soot control ability effectively reduces the soot oil pollution and abrasion, extends oil life, the excellent anti-abrasion and anti-oxidation capacity, even under bad working conditions can also provides excellent lubrication protection and prolong the service life of the engine, the good low-temperature fluidity can reduce start-up time and abrasion, it also saves the fuel,  it has a good compatibility with EGR and SCR, it also plays a very important role in  protecting rear exhaust treatment system, reducing harmful gas emissions and protecting the environment.
适用范围：适用所有进口和国产高速四冲程重负荷涡轮增压国Ⅲ、国Ⅳ和欧Ⅳ液化气、天然气及柴油车，特别适用于采用EGR、SCR尾排处理装置的各种国产、进口液化气、天然气以及柴油发动机驱动的载重车型发动机润滑。
