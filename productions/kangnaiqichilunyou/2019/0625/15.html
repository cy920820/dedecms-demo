GL-5 康耐琪合成车辆齿轮油 
Syntheic Gear Oil Of Vehicle
极压抗磨   传动平稳  四季通用
产品配方：聚α烯烃、Ⅲ类合成基础油、GL-5复合剂、特种粘指剂、其他辅料等，所有原辅材料全部进口采购。
产品性能：超越API GL-5 ，为为车辆齿轮组在高速低扭矩，低速高扭矩运行中提供非凡保护。出色的极压抗磨能力，令齿轮、驱动轴经久耐用；稳定的高低温性能，防止齿轮擦伤、磨损、并防止潮湿环境中齿轮组生锈，产生油泥，保持齿轮表面光滑清洁。特有的摩擦特性既降低燃油消耗，又提高了传动平稳性，换挡感觉轻松；极佳的极压性能避免了齿轮因磨损和点蚀造成的失效；杰出的抗氧化性能避免了因杂质沉积造成的密封件损坏；优异的防锈防腐性，有效保护轴承、同步器等部件免腐蚀低。优异的抗泡性，保持油膜完整，避免冲击破坏；优异的低温流动性，适用各种严酷环境。
Product performance:Beyond API GL-5 provides extraordinary protection for vehicle gear group in a high-speed low torque, low-speed and high torque operation. The excellent extreme pressure anti-abrasion ability makes gear and drive shaft durable, the stable performance at high and low temperature can prevent gear abrasion, wear, and prevent the gear group rust and sludge in the moisture environment, it keeps the gear surface smooth and clean, the peculiar  friction characteristics not only reduce fuel consumption but also improve the stability of the transmission and makes it easier to change shift, the excellent extreme pressure properties avoid the failure which is caused by abrasion and pitting gear, the excellent anti-oxidation performance avoids seals damages caused by  impurity deposition, the excellent anti-rust and corrosion performance effectively protect the bearing and synchronizer and other components from rusting, the excellent anti-foaming performance  maintains the integrity of the oil slick and avoids impact damage. Its excellent low temperature fluidity adjust to a variety of harsh environments.
适用规格：API GL-5标准和MAN342 SL、SCANIA STO 1:0、ZF TE-ML 05B-07A-12B-16F、DAF等规格。
适用范围：各种车型的手动变速箱、后桥、传动轴和非同步式变速箱润滑油。
注意事项：     ♥本品与其他润滑油、添加剂混合时其预期使用寿命将会缩短，并有损于性能。
